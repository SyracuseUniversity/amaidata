_School of Education_  
3 credit(s) At least 1x fall or spring

* * *

historical influences and current practices. Basic course for students
planning further study in higher education.

_School of Education_

_School of Education_  
3 credit(s) At least 1x fall or spring

* * *

Crosslisted with EDU 616

any, exposure to research methods. Quantitative and qualitative methods are
studied in the context of education.  

  
|

  * ### HED 617 - Intercollegiate Athletics in Higher Education

_School of Education_  
3 credit(s) Irregularly

* * *

The emergence of intercollegiate athletics in higher education. The academic,
socio-cultural, economic, and political benefits and costs. Reform
initiatives, athletic governance, and oversight processes impacting athletic
policies, advising practices, and support services.  

  
|

  * ### HED 618 - College Student Athletes

_School of Education_  
3 credit(s) Irregularly

* * *

Understanding college student-athletes based on theories of development and
college student diversity including gender, sexual orientation, race,
ethnicity, disability, international status and preparation. Complexities,
challenges and opportunities for student-athletes. Examination of eligibility,
advising and support practices.  

  
|

seminar is required. Instructor consent required.
